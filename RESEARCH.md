**Commoji** is not just “prettier” Git history – it's a **psychology-optimized** system proven to reduce cognitive load and accelerate scanning in dense commit logs.

### Key Benefits (backed by eye-tracking & real-world studies)

- **Instant visual scanning** – Emojis are detected **parafoveally** (before you even look directly at them), increase skipping of nearby text, and shorten total reading time on target words.  
  → Barach et al. (2021)

- **Faster processing & better understanding** – Congruent emojis make messages subjectively easier and quicker to read than plain text.  
  → Daniel & Camp (2020); Boutet et al. (2021)

- **Real-world speed in software teams** – Emoji prefixes in GitHub commits and PRs lead to **measurably faster team responses** and clearer organization.  
  → Rong et al. (2022)

### The 5 Psychological Principles That Power Commoji

1. **Visual Indexing & Attentional Capture** (Treisman's Feature Integration Theory + eye-tracking)  
   Emojis act as high-contrast pre-attentive anchors – your brain spots them instantly during skimming.

2. **Semantic Orthogonality**  
   Noun (tag) + verb separation eliminates redundancy and builds instant mental models.

3. **Gestalt Grouping & Chunking**  
   Emoji + tag creates a single perceptual “chunk” – easier to process than plain text lines.

4. **Cognitive Load Reduction & Processing Fluency**  
   Predictable structure offloads mental effort; once learned, reading feels effortless.

5. **Dual-Layer Signaling**  
   Emoji + matching tag reinforces meaning without overload (multimodal encoding).

> **Result**: Dense Git histories become scannable at a glance – exactly what developers need.
